Simple Simon Game

-- Directions --

Click the "START" button to begin a new game.

Simon will pick a colored button. It will flash so you know which one.

You will then have the chance to mimic exactly the button Simon picked.

If you guessed correctly, Simon will repeat his previous choice(s) and add a new one at the end of each round

Keep entering the Simon's sequence correctly to advance to the next round

GAME OVER!
